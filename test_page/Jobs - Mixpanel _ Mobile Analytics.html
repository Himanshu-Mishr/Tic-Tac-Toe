Helping the world learn from its data

Mixpanel's mission is to help the world learn from its data. We help companies learn how people use their products and applications so they can grow their business. Every day we face and solve new challenges with scaling, reliability, design, and making our customers happy.

Our culture

  • We are a product, engineering, and design-led organization.

  • Design is a critical part of our product. We constantly raise the bar on user experience.

  • We have a solutions team that aims to delight customers daily. They are smart, fast, and help our customers every step of the way.

  • Our engineering team primarily writes code in Python, C/C++, and JavaScript.

  • Great things take time. Our projects start as simple as possible and we gradually increase their complexity to foster great ideas.

  • We always ship code the moment it is better than what is live on our site – no matter what.
